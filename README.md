## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a third-year B.E. Computer Science & Engineering student at the **University of Mumbai**, specializing in **IoT, Cybersecurity & Blockchain Technology**. My work sits at the intersection of hardware, intelligence, and security — I like taking a system apart to understand it, then rebuilding it smarter.

Most of what I know, I learned by shipping something rather than reading about it. Whether it's optimizing how edge devices allocate compute, securing a network, or experimenting with a new AI model, I default to building a working version first and refining from there.

Right now, I'm expanding across **Artificial Intelligence, Edge AI, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, and Blockchain** — with the goal of designing systems that are efficient, secure, and genuinely useful.
